Understanding Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital money that uses protocols based on cryptography to secure transactions. It became popular because it allows peer-to-peer payments without needing traditional banks or intermediaries. Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum rely on computer networks and IP addresses to transmit packets of transaction data securely and quickly. They use TCP protocols to ensure reliable and ordered delivery of these data packets across the internet.
