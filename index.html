Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is digital money that only exists online. Its not physical, but people still use it to buy, sell, or invest. One fun fact is that the first real Bitcoin purchase bought two pizzas! It became popular because it felt new and independent from banks. It shows how humans can agree to give value to something we can’t even see.

The Internet

The internet is a giant network that links billions of devices. It lets people message friends, watch videos, and share ideas. Underneath, it’s just data moving along paths between computers.

Vocab

  • Computing Device: A physical device that runs programs (like a phone or laptop).
  • Computer Network: A group of devices connected to share data.
  • Protocol: The rules that systems use to talk to each other.
  • Bandwidth: The max amount of data a system can send per second.
